Frequently Asked Questions

How much does fridge repair cost in Cape Town?

The cost of a fridge repair in Cape Town depends on the specific issue, such as a fridge compressor replacement or a fridge gas refill service. We offer affordable fridge repair across the Western Cape with transparent pricing. Contact us today for a reliable quote from a certified refrigeration technician.

Do you offer emergency refrigeration repair?

Yes, we provide 24 hour refrigeration repair in the Western Cape. Our emergency refrigeration technicians in Cape Town and surrounding areas are ready to dispatch for urgent freezer repair and commercial breakdowns to save your perishable stock. Before calling, check our diagnostic checklist:

Diagnostic Checklist
  1. What unit is it? (e.g., Domestic Fridge)
  2. What is the exact problem?
  3. Is the motor running? Unplug immediately if NO

Can you repair commercial refrigeration systems?

Absolutely. Our commercial fridge repair specialists handle everything from restaurant fridge repair to supermarket refrigeration repair. We service display fridges, under-counter chillers, and industrial refrigeration systems throughout the Western Cape area.
